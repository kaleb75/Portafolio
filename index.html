In collaboration with the ITC/IPT team in China and TAO in Taiwan, I led the implementation of an innovative Manufacturing Execution System (MES) that optimizes the printed circuit board assembly (PCBA) process in the SMT PCA area. This project not only provides real-time visibility into production but also enhances traceability and efficiency across our processes.

With a holistic approach, the MES integrates and harmonizes departments such as Quality Assurance (QA), Automated Optical Inspection (AOI), Process Engineering (PE), and Warehouse (WH), along with critical testing and inspection processes including In-Circuit Testing (ICT), Functional Boundary Testing (FBT), Outgoing Quality Control (OQC), Incoming Quality Control (IQC), Mechanical Engineering (ME), and various shipping quality checks.

This integration facilitates seamless communication and information flow across all stages of production, from material entry to final product delivery. As a result, we have achieved reduced downtime, improved product quality, and maximized customer satisfaction, setting a high standard in electronic product manufacturing.
